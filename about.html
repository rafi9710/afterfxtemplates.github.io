✦ Who I Am ✦
The Artist
Hi, I'm Mohammad Rafi
VFX & Motion Graphics Artist · Bengaluru, India

I'm a VFX & Motion Graphics Artist based in Bengaluru, and I've spent years bringing celebrations to life through motion. What started as a love for Adobe After Effects turned into a full studio dedicated to one thing — making your special moments unforgettable.

I specialise in South Indian celebration videos — baby name reveals, cradle & naming ceremonies, wedding invitations, half saree functions and more, with full support for both Telugu and English. Every project is built with attention to detail, cinematic quality, and a personal touch.

Through my YouTube channel and this site, I share ready-to-use After Effects project files so creators at every level — beginners to professionals — can craft beautiful invitations of their own. Each template is made with care, so it feels personal, not generic.
